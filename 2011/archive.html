13-15TH MAY

NETAUDIO

Archive_

Over the weekend of 13-15th May 2011, Netaudio presented an engaging and interactive festival programme spanning across the four strands of live music, sound art, conference and broadcast. A total of 12 events were held at the London venues: the Roundhouse, KOKO, Cafe OTO, the Apiary Studios and  online.

  • NAL11 pdf report
  • NAL11 festival website

In 2008 Netaudio London took over the Shunt Lounge for a 4 day festival.  The festival programme included eight audio-visual installations, 45 live performances from a total of 81 artists involved. The festival attracted just under 3000 visitors.

  • Netaudio08 pdf report

2006 saw the first issue of London’s Netaudio festival. Over 3 days in September Netaudio’06 featured over 50 performers, commissioned a live audio-installation as well as various workshops and network discussions. Netaudio’06 took place at the Candid Arts Gallery, the Electrowerkz nightclub and the Pool Bar. It attracted some 900 visitors.
